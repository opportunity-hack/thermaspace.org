ThermaSpace Privacy Policy

ThermaSpace does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party. All thermal images, room data, snapshots, and temperature histories are stored only on your headset, inside the app's private storage, and are deleted when you clear them in-app or uninstall the app.

The optional "Share" feature, off by default, lets you view your own data from another device on your own local network; it requires a one-time code shown in the headset, sends data to no one else, and switches off automatically when you leave the app.

Room-scan data is accessed via Meta's Spatial Data permission solely to attach thermal imagery to your walls and never leaves the device.

Since no data is collected, there is nothing for the developer to delete; uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

This website (thermaspace.org) — not the app — uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors find the site and which pages are useful: page views, clicks, and file downloads. No advertising features are enabled, and none of this involves the app or your headset. You can opt out with any content blocker or the Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

Mount orders are processed by Stripe on Stripe's checkout page; this site never sees your payment details. We receive only what's needed to fulfill the order — your name, shipping address, email, and mount options.
